Island Activities

Ocean Adventures

Explore the most biodiverse marine ecosystem on the planet.

Scuba Diving

Explore coral walls, caverns, and shipwrecks guided by our PADI-certified instructors. All levels welcome.

Snorkeling Safari

Drift over vibrant house reefs teeming with clownfish, parrotfish, rays, and sea turtles in their natural habitat.

From $35 / person

Speedboat Safari

Island-hop across the atolls on a full-day speedboat adventure, stopping at sandbanks and local islands.

Water Sports

Jet skiing, windsurfing, wakeboarding, and kayaking — adrenaline meets paradise at our water sports center.

Above The Waves

Sunset Cruise

Sail the Indian Ocean aboard a traditional Dhoni as the sky turns to fire — with drinks and canapes on board.

Night Fishing

From $50 / person

Kayak Exploration
