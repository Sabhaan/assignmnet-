✦ Who We Are

Passionate About the Ocean Since 2009

Velaa Maldives was founded by a team of ocean enthusiasts, local guides, and conservation advocates who believed that travel should leave both traveler and destination better than before.

We partner exclusively with eco-conscious resorts, support local fishing communities, and contribute a portion of every booking to coral reef restoration projects across the atolls.

Travel That Gives Back

We believe that experiencing the beauty of the Maldives comes with a responsibility to protect it. For every guest who travels with us, we plant 10 coral fragments in our reef nursery and donate to local school programs.
